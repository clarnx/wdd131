Laptop Arena

The name Laptop Arena was selected to represent a simple and easy-to-remember laptop shop catering to tech enthusiasts, professionals, and students alike. It conveys a sense of expertise in the industry.

Optional domain availability: laptoparena.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as a landing page for a laptop shop, providing visitors with information about available laptop types. The website will highlight the best laptop options for different user needs while also offering an easy way for customers to reach out with inquiries.

Scenarios

  • Which laptop is best for gaming with high performance and a good graphics card?
  • What warranty options and discounts are available when purchasing from Laptop Arena?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Blue (#0047AB)
  • Secondary Color: Black (#000000)

Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat
  • Body Font: Oswald

Wireframe

  • Hero Section: Large banner showcasing latest laptop with a prominent CTA button.
  • Product Categories: Grid layout displaying different categories such as gaming, business, student, and creator laptops.
  • Key Features & Specs: Section with brief descriptions of laptop specifications.
  • Why Choose Us?: A list highlighting unique selling points.
  • Contact & Inquiry Form: A simple form for customers to ask questions.
